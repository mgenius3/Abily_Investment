Private Equity Management

Abily Investment LLC has distinguished itself through its Private Equity Management division, offering a unique and focused regional approach to investing in the MENA region and Emerging Markets. With a team of highly qualified professionals who bring a wealth of experience across various industries and sectors, Abily Investment is well-positioned to develop, invest in, and successfully exit private equity deals.

The private equity team at Abily Investment is comprised of individuals from diverse nationalities, providing invaluable "local" perspectives for deals across their target regions. This approach allows them to deeply understand the intricacies and opportunities present in the markets they operate in.

Abily Investment's primary focus revolves around acquiring controlling stakes in medium-sized enterprises within predominantly defensive sectors in the MENA region and Emerging Markets. This strategy is underpinned by their commitment to adding substantial value to the companies they invest in.

The team's collective experience spans a wide spectrum of financial expertise, including private equity, investment banking, transaction advisory, venture capital, financial research, strategy consulting, credit rating, and audit. This diverse skill set ensures a holistic approach to deal sourcing, execution, and management.

Furthermore, Abily Investment LLC has a strong cross-regional track record, which serves as a testament to their ability to navigate the intricacies of the markets they operate in. Their sectoral experience is equally varied, encompassing healthcare, education, real estate, shipping, petrochemicals, financial services, and logistics, providing them with a comprehensive understanding of the industries in which they invest.

By combining a well-qualified and diverse team, a laser focus on defensive sectors, and a successful track record across the MENA region and Emerging Markets, Abily Investment LLC's Private Equity Management division stands out as a formidable player in the realm of private equity, setting them apart and ensuring their continued success in the dynamic world of investments.
