Fraud Awareness

Dear Valued Clients and General public, We at Abily Investment LLC are committed to ensuring the security and trust of our clients. It has come to our attention that fraudulent activities and impersonation attempts have been on the rise, which is why we want to provide you with comprehensive information and guidance to protect yourself from potential scams and impersonations associated with our name.

Important Notice:

  • Official Communication Channels:
    Our official email domain is @abilyinvestment.com , and we do not use any other email addresses for communication.
    We do not operate on social media platforms, including Facebook or any other social networking sites.
  • Beware of Phishing Emails::
    Always verify the sender's email address when you receive emails claiming to be from Abily Investment LLC.
    We will never request sensitive information such as your account passwords or Social Security numbers.
  • Verify Phone Calls:
    In cases where you receive phone calls from individuals claiming to represent Abily Investment LLC, verify their identity by contacting our official customer service line listed on our website. You can book an appointment to speak with us on our official local number (+974) 7003 3831 while our international number listed on our official website is open for general inquiries.
    We will never ask for personal or financial information over the phone without prior verification.
  • Protect Your Personal Information:
    Be cautious about sharing personal or financial information with anyone, even if they claim to be from Abily Investment LLC.
    Regularly monitor your financial accounts for unauthorized transactions
  • Official Website:
    Our official website is www.abilyinvestment.com. Make sure you are using the correct and secure website to access your account or obtain information about our services.
  • Report Suspicious Activity:
    If you suspect any fraudulent activity or have received suspicious communications, please report it to our customer service team immediately.
    Additionally, report any such incidents to your local authorities and relevant cybercrime units.
  • Protecting Your Investments:
    Abily Investment LLC takes the security of your investments seriously. We have robust security measures in place to safeguard your assets, and we continuously monitor for any unusual activity.
  • Regulatory Compliance:
    We comply with all relevant regulations in Qatar and the United Kingdom and are dedicated to providing financial services in accordance with the law.
  • Customer Education:
    We encourage all clients to stay informed about potential fraud risks and follow best practices for online and financial security.
Note: Please remember that Abily Investment LLC is dedicated to the highest standards of professionalism, security, and client service. We are committed to protecting your investments and your personal information. If you have any doubts or questions about the authenticity of any communication you receive from Abily Investment LLC, please reach out to our customer service team at info@abilyinvestment.com. Your safety and trust are our top priorities. Thank you for your continued trust in Abily Investment LLC, and together, we can combat fraudulent activities and protect your financial well-being.
